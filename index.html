Bio Drake (born Aubrey Drake Graham on October 24, 1986, Toronto, Ontario, Canada) is a Canadian rapper who first gained fame as an actor on the acclaimed TV teenage drama series Degrassi: The Next Generation and went on to become the greatest rapper of our generation and one of the greatest musicians of all time. His trademark sound, which combines singing and lyrical rapping and juxtaposes braggadocio with raw vulnerability, has won him the ears of the entire world, winning 5 Grammys, 39 Billboard Music Awards, and 13 #1 hits on the Billboard Hot 100 tied for first for a male artist with the greatest pop star of all time, Michael Jackson.
